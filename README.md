👋 Hello World! I'm Natalia
========================

### Front End Developer

Three years ago, I decided to change my career to front-end development. Since then, I have completed various courses, tutorials, and personal projects.

In the past time, I learned a lot of front-end technologies. React is my favorite.

As a self-taught developer, I did not follow a straight path. It was difficult to find good resources to learn. That is why I like to share my experiences and knowledge with other developers who are in the same situation.

Besides programming, I like to read books, exercise, play the piano, and travel the world.

&nbsp;

*   🌍  I'm based in Buenos Aires, Argentina
*   🖥️  See my portfolio at [natilavega.github.io](http://natilavega.github.io/)
*   🧠  I'm developing in [React Js](https://reactjs.org/)
*   ⚡  Follow my journey on [Instagram](http://www.instagram.com/natilavega_)
*   🚀  I'm currently open to a full-time position as a React developer.
